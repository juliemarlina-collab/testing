CAMP21 Design System

Introduction to Design Thinking

Welcome to the CAMP21 Innovation Sprint. Madam Julie here—before we dive into solving complex challenges, let's establish the foundational mindset required to build human-centric solutions.

01

What is Design Thinking?

Design Thinking is a non-linear, iterative process that teams use to understand users, challenge assumptions, redefine problems, and create innovative solutions to prototype and test.

02

The CAMP21 Objective

Over the next 4 days, your team will progress through a rapid innovation cycle. You will utilize 14 structured templates to capture data, brainstorm ideas, and ultimately present an 8-slide Final Pitch to the judging panel.

03

The 5 Core Phases

The journey is split into five distinct actions: EMPATHY, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test. Each phase unlocks new tools to help you build a stronger prototype.
